Why Sunday Feast?

DJ Khamusique dreamed of being a celebrity, but life showed him a greater purpose. Through soulful deep house, food, and drinks, Sunday Feast is his gift to Bethlehem township: a space for joy, connection, and community.

Khamusique is a dynamic DJ and producer from Bethlehem, Free State, South Africa. His journey into music began in 2012, when he first stepped behind the decks and discovered his passion for creating soulful, deep, and energetic house experiences.

Over the years, Khamusique has built a strong reputation for delivering sets that blend deep, soulful, and modern house sounds, always leaving the crowd captivated and moving. His unique style is rooted in storytelling through music, drawing inspiration from his surroundings and the diverse cultures of South Africa.

From local stages in the Free State to performances across provinces and even beyond South Africa’s borders into Lesotho, Khamusique continues to push his brand and sound forward. His growth as an artist has also expanded into music production, where he channels his creativity into original projects that showcase his signature sound.

Dedicated, passionate, and always evolving, Khamusique is more than a DJ — he is a musical experience. With over a decade in the industry, his journey is just beginning, and the best is yet to come.
